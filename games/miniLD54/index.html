Boost Power (Mini Ludum Dare 54 - 2014)
Please allow Flash to work in order to play the web version.

Your hover racer can't stop and its energy is decreasing fast. The only way to survive is to use turbo boosts on the way and to achieve as much speed as you can!


You can only use the boost when your ship is right on top of an acceleration pad, so be precise and time it right to land exactly on it!


How long will you survive?

- Controls -

LEFT-RIGHT - Ship selection.

SPACE - Start / use turbo
