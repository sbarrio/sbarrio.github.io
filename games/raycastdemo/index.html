HaxeFlixel raycasting demo

A simple demo of raycasting rendering done in HaxeFlixel. It has textured walls, minimap (on desktop versions) enemy and item rendering and a weapon overlay.


Note: Runs significantly better on desktop builds when running on systems with a proper GPU.

- Controls -

WASD - Movement

LEFT/RIGHT - Look around

R - Toggle render modes
